What tests does our Web-App ?

We'll scan your resume in 30 seconds and evaluate it against a range of criteria that recruiters and hiring managers consider important.

Our resume test goes even deeper than an NLP scan, and gives you feedback on many other areas of your resume such as impact, soft skills and brevity. This enables you to identify gaps on your resume so you can fix them.

Optimization

Our SBERT-powered scanner finds key keywords from job descriptions to make your resume more ATS-friendly and increase your chances of getting shortlisted.

Spelling and grammar

Your resume must be free of spelling and grammar errors to impress hiring managers. Catch and fix mistakes to ensure a professional appearance.

Professional summary

A professional summary at the top of your resume grabs the hiring manager‚Äôs attention with a brief snapshot of your top skills and qualifications.

Measurable results

Our resume scanner checks your work history section for measurable accomplishments that demonstrate impressive achievements in past roles.

Word choice

Effective resumes use action verbs to make a powerful impact. Omit personal pronouns, filler words, and colloquial language for a professional tone.

Formatting

Our resume scanner verifies if your resume format is optimized for SBERT parsing by extracting and organizing key details from each section.

Skill Gap Analysis

Powered by SBERT, our system finds missing or weak skills in your resume compared to the job description and helps you improve them before applying.

Job Match Score

Our AI compares your resume with the job description and gives a Job Match Score, showing how well your skills and experience fit the role so you can quickly see where to improve.

Comprehensiveness

A comprehensive resume includes contact information, a professional summary, industry-relevant skills, work experience, and education.
