Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. My resume upload is failing — what should I do?

Ensure your resume is in one of the supported formats (.pdf, .doc, .docx, or .txt). If the issue persists, clear your cache and retry.

2. How accurate are the AI results?

Our AI model uses SBERT (Sentence-BERT) embeddings for contextual matching. While it’s highly accurate, it’s still an aid for decision-making — not a replacement for human judgment.

3. How do I reset my account or delete my data?

Email privacy@airesumescreening.com to request data deletion or account reset.
