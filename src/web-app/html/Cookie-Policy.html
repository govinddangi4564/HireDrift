Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how our platform uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, improve performance, and ensure secure functionality within the Intelligent Resume Screening (SBERT) web application.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, actions, and login sessions, making your interaction smoother and more personalized.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly, including login, authentication, and session management.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about system usage, page load times, and technical errors to enhance website reliability.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences such as dark/light mode or saved filters in the resume screening dashboard.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to analyze user interactions and improve model accuracy, without storing personal identifiers.
  • Security Cookies: Help detect and prevent fraudulent activity or unauthorized access.

3. Purpose of Using Cookies

We use cookies strictly to:

  • Maintain secure authentication and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Measure application performance and fix system issues.
  • Optimize user interface responsiveness.
  • Support language, theme, and accessibility preferences.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect functionality such as login sessions and data access.

  • For Chrome: Manage cookies in Chrome
  • For Firefox: Manage cookies in Firefox
  • For Edge: Manage cookies in Edge

5. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may integrate third-party analytics or security tools. These services may use cookies solely to measure performance or ensure compliance. We do not share personal data with external advertisers.
