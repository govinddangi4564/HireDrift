Help-Centre

Welcome to the Intelligent Resume Screening and Shortlisting using NLP platform. This User Guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to use our system effectively, helping candidates and recruiters make the most of the AI-powered resume analysis powered by Sentence-BERT (SBERT) embeddings.

Our system intelligently evaluates resumes, compares them with job descriptions, and ranks candidates based on semantic similarity and skill relevance. Using advanced NLP and ML algorithms, it streamlines recruitment by identifying top candidates efficiently.

Getting Started

Create an account, upload your resume (supported formats: .pdf, .doc, .docx, .txt), and access your personalized dashboard.

Uploading a Resume

Navigate to "Upload Resume", select your file, and the AI will extract key details like skills, experience, and achievements. Avoid password-protected files.

Job Description Matching

Upload or enter a job description. The AI computes a semantic similarity score using SBERT embeddings, showing how well your resume aligns with job requirements.

Analyzing Results

  • Skill Relevance: Matches between your skills and job requirements.
  • Experience Alignment: Fit of your professional background.
  • Keyword Coverage: Use of job-related terms.
  • Soft Skills: Tone and clarity evaluation.

Improving Your Resume

AI-generated suggestions help improve phrasing, keywords, and structure for better readability and alignment.

Recruiter Dashboard

Recruiters can upload multiple resumes, compare them with job descriptions, and view ranked candidates instantly.

Data Privacy & Security

Your data is encrypted and processed securely. Users can delete their data anytime.

Technical Foundation

Powered by Sentence-BERT (SBERT), which understands context beyond keywords, improving accuracy.
