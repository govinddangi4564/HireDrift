About us

This Privacy Policy describes how our AI-powered system handles, processes, and safeguards personal data used in intelligent resume screening, matching, and shortlisting.

Our platform leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Sentence-BERT (SBERT) to semantically analyze resumes and job descriptions. The system converts textual content into embeddings for efficient, bias-aware shortlisting ‚Äî ensuring a fair, transparent, and intelligent recruitment process.

1. Data Collection and Purpose

We collect minimal and relevant data for model processing and improvement. The types of data collected include:

  • Resume Content: Information extracted from uploaded resumes (skills, education, experience, etc.).
  • Job Descriptions: Data provided by recruiters or HR teams for role matching.
  • User Information: Names, emails, and company details necessary for authentication and communication.
  • System Metadata: Browser information, IP address, and timestamps for security and performance analytics.

All data is collected to improve the accuracy, transparency, and ethical behavior of our SBERT-based recommendation engine.

2. Use of Data in NLP Model Processing

Our system uses SBERT to transform text into dense vector representations, enabling deep semantic understanding of resumes and job requirements. This allows the platform to:

  • Identify candidates most semantically aligned with job requirements.
  • Reduce bias compared to keyword-based systems.
  • Continuously learn from anonymized performance data to improve fairness and accuracy.

Processing occurs automatically via our secured NLP pipeline. We do not use data for unrelated commercial purposes or unauthorized training.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Uploaded resumes and job data are stored securely in encrypted databases. Retention periods depend on user activity and legal requirements:

  • Active data is retained only while your account remains active or until deletion is requested.
  • Backup data may persist for up to 90 days after deletion to ensure system reliability.
  • All backups are encrypted and inaccessible to unauthorized parties.

4. Data Security Measures

We adopt industry-standard practices to protect personal data:

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256) during data transmission and storage.
  • Strict access control and multi-factor authentication for internal users.
  • Regular audits and penetration testing to identify vulnerabilities.
  • Secure data destruction protocols upon request or expiration.

5. Ethical Use of AI & Bias Mitigation

As an AI-powered platform, we understand the risks of algorithmic bias. Our system incorporates fairness evaluation metrics and bias detection layers to ensure diversity in candidate selection. The SBERT embeddings are regularly retrained using balanced datasets to minimize bias toward gender, ethnicity, or institution.

We also maintain human oversight ‚Äî AI suggestions are designed to assist, not replace, human hiring decisions.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies only for authentication, personalization, and analytics. They help track system usage, detect performance issues, and optimize user experience. You may disable cookies via browser settings without affecting basic functionality.

7. Third-Party Integrations

Our infrastructure is hosted on secure cloud providers compliant with ISO/IEC 27001 and GDPR standards. Third-party analytics tools (if used) collect only anonymized data for performance optimization. No personal data is shared or sold to advertisers.

8. User Rights and Controls

Users have the right to:

  • Access and download their stored data.
  • Request correction or deletion of personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for processing and request anonymization.
  • Complain to a data protection authority if rights are violated.

All requests are processed within 30 days via privacy@example.com.

9. Research and Model Improvement

For research and algorithmic improvement, anonymized datasets may be used to enhance the semantic understanding and fairness of SBERT models. These datasets do not contain identifiable user information and are handled under strict research ethics guidelines.

10. Policy Updates

This policy may be revised periodically to reflect updates in technology, data regulations, or system features. We notify users of significant changes via email or dashboard alerts. The latest version is always available on our website.
